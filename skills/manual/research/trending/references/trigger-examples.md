# Trigger Examples

Use these examples to test whether the `trending` skill triggers correctly.

## Positive (Chinese)

- 今天 AI 领域有什么新动态？
- 帮我汇总一下本周的 AI 产品发布和论文。
- 给我一份今天的 AI 趋势简报。
- 总结一下最近 Hacker News 和 Reddit 上的 AI 热点。

## Positive (English)

- Give me today's AI trend digest.
- What happened in AI this week?
- Summarize the latest ML papers and product launches from today.
- Show me the trending AI topics from GitHub and X.

## Negative / Near Miss

- How do I use the OpenAI API? (Specific usage question, not a trend digest)
- What is the latest version of React? (Specific software version, not an AI trend)
- Explain the Transformer architecture. (Concept explanation, not a trend digest)
- I want to learn machine learning, where should I start? (Learning plan request, use personal-growth-coach)
